ℹ️ How Overrides Work

🤖 AI Prediction Layer — normally calculates risk based on historical data + weather + time

👮 Human Override Layer — when you add an event, the AI prediction is paused for that road and commuters see the restriction instead

⏰ Auto Resume — after end time, normal AI prediction resumes automatically

This is Responsible AI — humans stay in control. AI handles patterns, humans handle exceptions.
